Team A

Guided Tour to the Casa da Musica

This mobile app aims to give its users a new way to visit "Casa da Música", through a set of 360º photos and movies.

This new way to interact with the space will provide a wide range of details and facts that one could not have in a real-time tour and show some of the multiple environments that it can deliver to the users.

The app will have a support website to provide the admins the possibility to add new rooms and edit content.

iTBirthday

Team D

Intelligent Sales Guide

The goal of the "Intelligent Sales Guide" project is to develop an intelligent sales system that helps the salesmen in asking effective questions to its clients, in order to better recommend appropriate products from a store.

This system selects sequences of questions to send to the salesmen based on the specificity of each question and on the effectiveness of similar sequences in the past in selling one or more recommended products.
